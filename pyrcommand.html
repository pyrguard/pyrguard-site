Command software

PyrCommand Alpha

Command software for wildfire planning, coordination, simulation, and after-action review.

Purpose

Purpose

PyrCommand is being developed as the command-software layer of the PyrGuard ecosystem. The Alpha prototype explores how wildfire-response teams could coordinate assets, evaluate containment strategies, track incident activity, and review operational outcomes in a structured interface.

Alpha scope

Current Alpha Capabilities

Incident simulation

Scenario workflows for exploring incident planning and response coordination concepts.

Containment objectives

Structured objective tracking for evaluating containment priorities and response intent.

Asset coordination

Prototype coordination views for crews, equipment, support assets, and operational resources.

Operations log

Chronological incident activity capture for review, context, and after-action workflows.

Tactical telemetry

Interface concepts for surfacing incident status, asset posture, and operational signals.

After-action review

Review workflows for examining objectives, logs, simulated outcomes, and operational decisions.

Mobile observer mode

Observer-oriented concepts for lightweight field visibility and incident updates.

Cloud incident session foundation

Early foundation for persistent incident sessions and shared operational context.

System role

Role in the PyrGuard System

PyrCommand is intended to support the broader PyrGuard infrastructure model by connecting suppression assets, operational plans, validation data, and incident workflows into a more coordinated response environment.

Status: Alpha prototype in active development. PyrCommand is not currently marketed as a certified emergency-response system or deployed operational platform.
